Who We Are

BLD Institute of Research & Learning Foundation

A Section 8 non-profit committed to advancing education, digital literacy, research, innovation, and skill development — empowering students, educators, and communities through technology-enabled learning.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive and digitally empowered society where every learner has access to quality education, innovation, and opportunities for growth.

Our Mission

Promote digital literacy, support quality education, encourage lifelong learning, build capacities of teachers and students, and contribute to national development through knowledge-driven initiatives.
